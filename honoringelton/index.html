Honoring Elton John

Come to know the Rocketman!

Who is the Rocketman?

Elton John is one of the world's greatest rock stars ever known. Born in 1947 under the name Reginald Dwight, he quickly was recognized for his impressive piano skills at the young age of three years old. Little Reggie knew there he was born to be a star, and a star he was. In his early twenties, he changed his name to Elton Hercules John, and took the world by suprsie with his music. Over the years, Elton has been recognized as one of the most influential musicians of the twentieth century. Even in his older age, Elton still produces music and even achieved a number one record in 2021, alongside Dua Lipa. His legacy continues to grow each year.

A Place to Honor

Come learn more about Elton John's work over the years, both inside and outside of the music industry. Elton has been involved in numerous soundtracks, some of which were revolutionary to the world of music. As a member of his community, Elton has gone out of his way to make a difference in many causes and charities, becoming a charitable philanthropist early on. Come honor his name and ongoing legacy!
