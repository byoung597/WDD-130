Overview

Purpose

I will build an informational website which educates the viewer on the cultural influences Elton John has made on the world during his ongoing career. It will open with a home page that will review Elton John's life as well as the overall intended purpose of the webpage. The there will be links to two other pages. One will review the work in philanthropy which Elton has accomplished. While the other will review a few of his famous soundtracks he has written. The overall purpose of the website will be to inform anyone about how talented and giving Elton John really is.
